Frequently Asked Questions:

What's Blazing?

Blazing is the website about reviewing movies and TV shows, made for both fans and critics.

How do I review a movie/TV show?

Before you review, you need to register an account. Make sure you supply enough information

After you registered and are logged in, click on a movie or TV show and enter your appropriate ratings. The submit-review form is located at the bottom of the details page.

I can't find the movie/TV show that I want to review.

Use the search bar at the top of the page and enter keywords of the movie/TV show.

What if I made a mistake to a review I just submitted?

You are allowed to edit or delete your review after you submit. It will show after you reload the page.

To edit, simply change the rating or edit the body of your review and click on the submit button.

To delete, simply click the 'x' button.

There is a review that I find inappropriate.

If you are logged in, you can report the review by clicking the flag button. Write a reason why you are reporting this review, and submit the report by clicking the submit button.

Your report will be sent to the administrator.
